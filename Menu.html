Menu

Our lunches are available with vegetarian/ non-vegetarian categories. A sample lunch menu is provided below: Children who stay full day are provided with a snack at 3.00pm before the end of our school day.


Special consideration is given to children with a special dietary requirement and suitable arrangements are made between the parents and school to ensure we observe this to suit individual children.


Our meals and snacks are well balanced and nutritious. We offer mid-morning snacks and milk to all children daily. A sample of a weekly mid-morning snack is provided below:


  • Monday: Milk and Cereal

  • Tuesdays: Fresh fruit and Milk

  • Wednesday: Hot Chocolate with Bread (with Honey)

  • Thursday: warm milk with doughnut/cupcakes

  • Fridays: Porridge oats/ banana
